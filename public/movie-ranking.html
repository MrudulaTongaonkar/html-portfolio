This movie makes us question every desicion we take . Its a beautiful fiction movie which will blow your mind.

This is am emotional romantic movie . At the end you will definately cry while watching this movie.

This ia a pyschological thriller . The plot for the movie is decided very beautifully and the end will terrify you .
